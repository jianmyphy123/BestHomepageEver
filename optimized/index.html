Make this my mobile homepage!

Set Homepage

Choose your browser

Internet Explorer

Step 1.

Click the Tools tab (ALT-T). Select Internet Options, at or near the bottom of the list.

Step 2.

Enter or copy/paste http://besthomepageever.com into homepage box. Be sure Start with home page is selected under 'Startup'.

Google Chrome

Step 1.

First, click the settings icon  in upper-right portion of browser. Then, choose "Settings."

Step 2.

Next, click to match the below settings.


Safari

Homepage Settings

Choose Safari > Preferences, then click General.

  • Set homepage

    Enter http://besthomepageever.com in the Homepage field, or click Set to Current Page if you are currently viewing BestHomepageEver.

  • Open new windows with homepage:

    Click the New windows open with pop-up menu, then choose Homepage.

  • Open new tabs with homepage:

    Click the New tabs open with pop-up menu, then choose Homepage.

    • Convenience tip: Add home button

    Click on the View Menu > Customize Toolbar

    In the dialog thats open drag and drop the Home button over the Toolbar.

Firefox

Set Homepage:

Click settings menu in far upper-right. Choose options. Then, copy/paste http://besthomepageever.com/ into Home Page box.

Above that bar, choose Show my home page in drop down menu.

Open new tabs with homepage: (recommended)

Click here to add extension.

Microsoft Edge

Step 1.

Click the ellipsis (...) menu in the upper right. Scroll down to the Settings button.

Step 2.

Under the heading "Open with", choose the radio button for "A specific page or pages", then from the pull down menu, choose "Custom".

Step 3.

Directly under the Custom pull down menu option, type in http://besthomepageever.com, then hit ENTER, +, or the save sign to submit.

Important:

You may need to remove any other pages listed by clicking the "X" to the right (i.e. MSN, about:start, etc.).
